# Success Metrics and Decision Rules

## 1. Success Metrics
| Metric ID | Metric | Target | How to Measure | Related Requirement |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| M-01 | Task completion rate | 80% or higher | Completed tasks / total tasks from T1-T5 | FR-01, FR-05, FR-06, FR-07, FR-08 |
| M-02 | Artwork discovery success rate | 80% or higher | Users who find artwork matching their preferred style, fandom, or tag / total users | FR-05, FR-06 |
| M-03 | Search and filter success rate | 80% or higher | Users who successfully narrow results by style, fandom, or critique status / total users | FR-06 |
| M-04 | Artwork detail understanding | 75% or higher | Users who can explain the artwork details, tags, and critique information / total users | FR-07, FR-08 |
| M-05 | Usability rating | Average 4 out of 5 | Feedback form rating after prototype test | FR-13 |
| M-06 | Feature usefulness rating | Average 4 out of 5 | Feedback form rating for gallery, tags, artwork detail, and critique features | F-03, F-04 |
| M-07 | User confusion rate | 30% or lower | Users who hesitate, click the wrong place, or ask for help / total users | FR-13 |

## 2. Decision Rules
| Result | Decision |
|---|---|
| Metrics meet target | Continue developing the current IceCream MVP direction. |
| Some metrics fail | Revise gallery filters, tag labels, artwork detail layout, or critique indicators before implementation. |
| Most metrics fail | Revisit the experiment objective, user stories, and MVP feature scope before building the final prototype. |
