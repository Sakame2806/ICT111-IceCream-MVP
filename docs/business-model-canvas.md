# Business Model Canvas - IceCream

| BMC Block | IceCream Project Application |
|---|---|
| **Customer Segments** | Campus digital artists, illustrators, comic creators, hobbyist creators, and art club members. |
| **Value Proposition** | A dedicated web-based community platform to showcase artworks, discover peers with similar interests, and receive structured, constructive feedback rather than simple reactions. |
| **Channels** | Campus art clubs, student group chat links (e.g., Telegram, Line), social media sharing, word-of-mouth among artists. |
| **Customer Relationships** | Self-service platform, peer-to-peer critique culture, creator dashboards, and community guidelines for constructive feedback. |
| **Revenue / Sustainability** | Currently focused on community building without direct monetization. Future sustainability could come from premium features, sponsorships, or ads. |
| **Key Resources** | Web interface/platform, backend database (Firebase/Supabase), user-generated content (artworks), and an active community of creators. |
| **Key Activities** | Platform development and maintenance, moderating community content, categorizing artworks, and developing critique features. |
| **Key Partners** | Campus art clubs, student councils, university lecturers/advisors, and influential student artists. |
| **Cost Structure** | Web hosting and domain costs, database storage costs, development and maintenance time, and marketing/communication materials. |
