# Initial IT Venture Idea Log
## Idea 1
### Problem Area
 Visitors want to share how they got to the attractions and what their experience was like.
### Target Users
 Tourists who have no idea and information for their trip. 
### Current Alternative
 They ask their friend or visit to Tourism authorities offical website of the country for information. 
### Proposed IT Solution
a Web2 website or a mobile app.
### Possible Technology
HTML/CSS/JS prototype, database or Google Sheet backend, image upload feature, search/filter function.
### Why This is Suitable
The system can be prototyped using web development, database design, UI/UX design, and basic validation with real tourists.

## Idea 2
### Problem Area
 Artists want a community to share and discuss their works.
### Target Users
 Digital Visual Artists
### Current Alternative
 Creating a group in an IM like Telegram or Line then invite artists to join that group.
### Proposed IT Solution
 a Web2 website or a mobile app.
### Possible Technology
HTML/CSS/JS prototype, database or Google Sheet backend, image upload feature, search/filter function.
### Why This is Suitable
The system can be prototyped using web development, database design, UI/UX design, and provide artists with the most basic social functions.
