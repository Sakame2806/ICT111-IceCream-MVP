## Overall Risk Decision
The IceCream prototype is **safe to continue building with mitigation**. The project has a clear responsible design direction, but several items must be fixed before final implementation: remove unnecessary sign-up fields, avoid real sensitive data, replace third-party/Pixiv-branded assets, validate upload and comment forms, restrict moderator actions, and improve tag validation. These risks should remain open until the team creates GitHub issues or commits showing the mitigation work.
